LIVING SACRIFICE AND THE ALTAR Hey there! My name is Gauri, I’m very excited to share my first blog on my website page. I’m so sure you’re gonna love it. There are many things, incidents or people in our lives for sure, by which we indivisual learns a lot and add value in our lives. Here I would be sharing some of the most important values that every other person in the world covets. My blogs can be based on very simple examples to really high value attached examples, which anyone and everyone can relate to. Past few weeks back I learnt about some really power revelation of “Living Sacrifice and The Altar”. In simple language “HOW A DISCIPLINED LIFE LEADS TO THE ETERNLE DELIGHT” for which whole world run towards. Let’s start to go to find the deeper knowledge about it. The first word I’ll take is ALTAR then LIVING SACRIFICE and then PRIEST and their meaning or what they presents. 1 Altar- The place designed to build a personal or intimate relationship with God. 2 Sacrifice- Our living bodies, bringing it to the altar INTENSIONALLY ( kind of discipline ). 3 Priest- The person who used to be the mediator between men and God,and used to offer Sacrifices on the altar behalf of people’s sins. So these were the instructions were given to a priest by God in the ancient times that FIRE should always be burning on the altar, and keep fire burning on the altar he had to put WOOD continually. So the ancient secene is to keep ourselves pure and presentable before God, the chosen priest by God had to do it for the people. And it was priest’s duty to keep the Fire burning on the altar. By doing this God used to forgive their sins,and that fire used to sent by God ,which used to consume the sacrifice, meaning that offering is accepted by the Lord. Now we know how it used to be before Christ, But in new era ( after Christ ) it has different revelation of the ALTAR,SACRIFICE (LIVING),PRIEST,FIRE and WOOD. Although its very old tradition kind of thing but it has very significant value attached to it. Now we don’t do exactly all thing old way but new way. Let’s understand it how? In new time means after Christ all these things represents same value but different objects with the same meaning, like 3 of them I have already explained now Fire - Holy Sprit, that baptized us with heavenly fire. Wood- Our Prayer life, which has to be consistent.
